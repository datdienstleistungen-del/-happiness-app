# Company

## Purpose
Core company information.

## Description
Happiness is a European social community platform and AI Creator Operating System founded to provide an independent, privacy-respecting alternative to US-dominated social media and creator tools.

## Key Facts
- European-hosted (GDPR compliant).
- All data stays in Europe.
- Open to all creators across Europe.
- Built for multiple languages (German, English, Spanish, French, Italian, Dutch, Greek).
- Free tier available, Premium subscription for unlimited AI access.

## Internal Rules
- Never misrepresent the platform as "just another social network".
- Happiness is an AI-first creator platform with social features, not a social network with AI features.
- Always emphasize the European value proposition when relevant.
