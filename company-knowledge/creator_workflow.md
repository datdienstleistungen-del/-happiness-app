# Creator Workflow

## Purpose
Document the complete creator journey on Happiness.

## The Complete Workflow
1. **Idea** — A creator has an idea for content.
2. **Creator Engine** — AI helps develop the idea into a full content package.
3. **NCG Academy** — AI reviews and provides improvement feedback.
4. **Refinement** — Creator iterates based on AI feedback.
5. **Community Feedback** — Creator shares draft in community for human feedback.
6. **Final Polish** — Creator applies both AI and community insights.
7. **Publish** — Content is published on Happiness and optionally cross-posted.
8. **Analytics** — Performance is tracked and analyzed.
9. **Learn** — AI explains what worked and suggests future improvements.
10. **Repeat** — Each cycle makes the creator better.

## Key Principle
Every step adds value. AI handles the heavy lifting. Community provides the human touch. The creator remains in control.
