# Vision

## Purpose
Describe the long-term vision for Happiness.

## Content
Happiness will become the default operating system for European creators.

A single platform where:
- Ideas become content.
- Content becomes learning.
- Learning becomes publishing.
- Publishing becomes growth.
- Growth becomes community.

All powered by an intelligent AI that knows the creator, the platform, and the audience.

We envision a future where every European creator — from hobbyist to professional — has access to world-class AI tools, community feedback, and a publishing ecosystem — without handing their data to US or Chinese corporations.
