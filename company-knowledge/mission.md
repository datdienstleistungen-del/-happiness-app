# Mission

## Purpose
Define the core mission of Happiness.

## Content
Happiness is building Europe's first AI Creator Operating System.

Our mission is to empower every creator in Europe to transform ideas into professional content, learning, publishing, and growth — using AI as a collaborative partner, not a replacement.

We believe:
- Every person has something valuable to create.
- AI should lower the barrier, not remove the human.
- Europe needs its own creator ecosystem — independent, privacy-respecting, GDPR-compliant.
- Community and AI together create the best outcomes.
