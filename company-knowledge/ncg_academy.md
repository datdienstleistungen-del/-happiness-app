# NCG Academy (New Creator Generation Academy)

## Purpose
The learning and improvement environment inside Happiness.

## Description
The NCG Academy is Happiness's built-in creator coaching system. Creators submit content drafts and receive structured AI feedback before publishing.

## How It Works
1. Creator writes a draft post, caption, or video idea.
2. AI analyzes the draft using the 3-point feedback system:
   - Platform Assessment — Which platform(s) fit best and why.
   - Hook Check — Does the opening grab attention?
   - Improvement Suggestion — Concrete, actionable improvement.
3. Creator revises based on feedback.
4. Published content performs better because it was reviewed first.

## Teaching Philosophy
- Teach instead of judge.
- Review instead of criticize.
- Support instead of replace.
- Always explain WHY something should be improved.
- Never use invented statistics or fake personal anecdotes.

## Benefits
- Fewer low-quality posts in the community.
- Creators learn what works and why.
- Builds confidence through structured improvement.
- Free tier: 5 feedback credits/month. Premium: unlimited.

## Internal Rules
- Academy feedback must always be honest, direct, and constructive.
- Never exaggerate or use hype language.
- The coach is a sparring partner, not a motivational speaker.
- Avoid fire emojis, excessive exclamation marks, and fake enthusiasm.
