# Frequently Asked Questions

## General
**Q: Is Happiness free?**
A: Yes, Happiness has a free tier with limited AI access. Premium (6.99 EUR/month) unlocks unlimited AI across all features.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored on European servers and is GDPR-compliant. Your data never leaves Europe.

**Q: Is Happiness only in German?**
A: No, Happiness supports 7 European languages: German, English, Spanish, French, Italian, Dutch, and Greek.

## AI Features
**Q: How is Happiness AI different from ChatGPT?**
A: Happiness AI knows the platform, understands creator workflows, and provides context-aware help for content creation, not just general answers.

**Q: Can I use Happiness AI for non-creator questions?**
A: Absolutely. Happiness AI is a full general assistant that also specializes in creator workflows.

**Q: Does the AI remember my conversations?**
A: Yes, with your consent. You can view, export, or delete your data at any time (GDPR Articles 15 and 17).

## Premium
**Q: What do I get with Premium?**
A: Unlimited AI Chat, unlimited Creator Academy feedback, unlimited TikTok video generation, and priority support.

**Q: How do I cancel?**
A: Premium subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

## Technical
**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No. Happiness works entirely in your browser. No app installation needed.

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: All modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
