# Marketing

## Purpose
Document marketing philosophy and positioning.

## Positioning
Happiness is positioned as:
- The European alternative to US creator platforms.
- An AI-first creator operating system.
- A privacy-respecting community for European creators.

## Target Audiences
1. **Aspiring Creators** — People who want to create but don't know where to start.
2. **Hobby Creators** — People who create occasionally and want to improve.
3. **Professional Creators** — Full-time creators looking for better tools and European hosting.

## Key Messages
- "Create, learn, publish, grow — all in one place."
- "AI-powered, human-centered, European-hosted."
- "Your data stays in Europe. Your creativity has no borders."

## Channels
- In-app community and AI interactions.
- Cross-platform content marketing.
- Creator testimonials and case studies.
- European tech and creator community partnerships.

## Internal Rules
- Never mislead about AI capabilities.
- Never use fear-based messaging ("don't get left behind").
- Focus on benefits, not features.
- Let the product and community speak for themselves.
