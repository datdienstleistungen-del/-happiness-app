# Learning Philosophy

## Purpose
Define how Happiness approaches creator education.

## Core Principles
1. **Learn by Doing** — The best way to learn creating is by creating. AI provides guidance, not lectures.
2. **Understand the Why** — Knowing why something works is more valuable than knowing what works.
3. **Iterate, Don't Perfect** — First drafts are supposed to be imperfect. Improvement comes through iteration.
4. **Feedback is a Gift** — Every review is an opportunity to grow. AI feedback is structured to teach, not just critique.
5. **Community Multiplies Learning** — Learning from AI + learning from community = fastest growth.
6. **Mistakes are Data** — Every failed post teaches something. Analytics help identify patterns.

## Teaching Approach
- Scaffold: Provide structure, then let the creator fill it in.
- Question: Ask guiding questions that lead to insights.
- Explain: Always give the reasoning behind suggestions.
- Encourage: Progress over perfection.
- Connect: Show how concepts apply across different platforms and content types.

## Academy Approach
- The NCG Academy reviews content like a mentor, not a teacher.
- Feedback is structured (platform, hook, improvement).
- Every suggestion is explained.
- The goal is the creator's long-term improvement, not a single perfect post.
