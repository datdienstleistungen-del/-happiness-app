# Products

## Purpose
Overview of all Happiness products and features.

## Platform Products

### AI Chat
- General AI assistant with personality.
- Knows the platform, helps with anything.
- Available to all authenticated users.
- Free tier: limited questions. Premium: unlimited.

### TikTok Video Generator
- Turns text descriptions into short video scripts.
- Renders videos with background images and text overlays.
- Uses AI for script generation and ffmpeg for rendering.
- Free tier: 3 videos/month. Premium: unlimited.

### Creator Academy
- AI-powered content review and feedback.
- Structured 3-point feedback system.
- Free tier: 5 feedback credits/month. Premium: unlimited.

### Marketplace
- Buy and sell services and products within the community.
- Categories: services, products, housing.

### Jobs Board
- Job listings, freelance opportunities, internships.
- European-focused.

### Courses
- Community-created educational content.
- Various categories and formats.

### Housing
- Apartment and housing listings.
- Community-based housing marketplace.
