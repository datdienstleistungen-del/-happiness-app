# Future Roadmap

## Purpose
Document planned features and strategic direction.

## Short-term (Next 3 Months)
- Enhanced Creator Engine with multi-platform publishing.
- Improved AI agent architecture.
- Community analytics dashboard.
- Mobile app (iOS/Android).
- Expanded language support.

## Medium-term (3-9 Months)
- AI-powered content scheduling and automation.
- Collaborative creator projects.
- Advanced analytics with trend detection.
- API for third-party integrations.
- Creator marketplace expansion.

## Long-term (9-18 Months)
- Full AI Creator Operating System.
- Cross-platform content synchronization.
- AI mentor network (learn from successful creators).
- European creator grants and funding.
- Decentralized identity and reputation system.

## Strategic Principles
- Always maintain European data sovereignty.
- AI augments, never replaces human creativity.
- Community governance by creators, for creators.
- Open standards where possible, proprietary where necessary for quality.
- Sustainable growth over venture-capital-driven expansion.
