# Publishing

## Purpose
Document publishing philosophy and features.

## Publishing Philosophy
- Publish on Happiness first, cross-post to other platforms.
- Content should be platform-appropriate but maintain creator identity.
- AI assists with platform optimization without losing creator voice.

## Platform Support
- **Happiness Feed**: Native posting with text and images.
- **TikTok**: Short-form video content (via TikTok Video Generator).
- **Instagram**: Visual content with optimized captions.
- **YouTube**: Long-form video descriptions and scripts.
- **LinkedIn**: Professional content formatting.

## Best Practices
- Each platform has unique audience expectations.
- Hooks matter most in the first 1-2 seconds/lines.
- Authenticity outperforms polish on most platforms.
- Call-to-action increases engagement significantly.
