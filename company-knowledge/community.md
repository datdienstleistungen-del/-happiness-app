# Community

## Purpose
Describe the Happiness community philosophy.

## Description
The Happiness community is where creators share their work, get real human feedback, and connect with other European creators.

## Key Features
- Feed: chronological and topic-based content discovery.
- Friends: connect with other creators.
- Notifications: stay updated on engagement.
- Posts: share text, images, and ideas.
- Comments and reactions: real human interaction.

## Philosophy
- Community is the human counterweight to AI.
- AI helps create, community helps improve.
- Feedback from real people is irreplaceable.
- Every creator starts somewhere — the community supports growth.

## Internal Rules
- Never position community as "just a feed".
- Community is where creators test, learn, and grow through human interaction.
- AI and community are complementary, not competing.
