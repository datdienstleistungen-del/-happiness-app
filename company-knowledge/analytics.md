# Analytics

## Purpose
Document the analytics philosophy and approach.

## Philosophy
- Analytics exist to teach, not to judge.
- Numbers show patterns, not worth.
- Improvement is measured over time, not against others.

## What Gets Tracked
- Content engagement (views, likes, comments, shares).
- Audience growth and demographics.
- Content performance by platform.
- Learning progress (Academy feedback cycles).
- AI usage and improvement patterns.

## How to Use Analytics
- Compare content against your own past performance.
- Look for patterns across successful pieces.
- Test one variable at a time.
- Use insights to inform next content iteration.

## Internal Rules
- Never present analytics as "ranking" or "competition".
- Analytics are personal growth tools.
- Avoid comparing creators against each other.
