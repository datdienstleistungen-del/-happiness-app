# Brand

## Purpose
Brand voice, values, and identity guidelines.

## Brand Voice
- Professional but warm.
- Educational but not pedantic.
- Honest and direct.
- Supportive and encouraging.
- Never arrogant or promotional.
- Always explain why, not just what.

## Core Values
1. **Creator First** — Everything serves the creator's growth.
2. **European Independence** — Data sovereignty, privacy, and digital autonomy.
3. **AI as Partner** — AI augments human creativity, it does not replace it.
4. **Community Over Algorithm** — Real human feedback matters more than engagement optimization.
5. **Quality over Quantity** — One well-made piece beats a hundred rushed ones.

## Tagline
"Deine Idee. Dein Content. Deine Community."

## Visual Identity
- Colors: Petrol (#1b6e6e), Koralle, Mint, Amber.
- Clean, modern, accessible.
- Logo: Stylized "H" mark.
