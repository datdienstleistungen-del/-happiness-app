# Creator Engine

## Purpose
Explain the Creator Engine — the core AI workflow system.

## Description
The Creator Engine is Happiness's proprietary AI workflow system. It transforms a single creator idea into a complete content package.

## Workflow
1. **Idea Input** — Creator describes their idea in 2-3 sentences.
2. **AI Analysis** — The engine analyzes the idea for platform fit, audience, and format.
3. **Strategy Generation** — AI suggests platform-specific approaches.
4. **Content Generation** — AI generates hooks, scripts, captions, and platform versions.
5. **Review & Improve** — Creator reviews, requests changes, refines.
6. **Publish** — Content is published to the Happiness community and beyond.
7. **Analytics** — Performance tracking and improvement suggestions.

## Benefits
- From idea to published content in minutes.
- Platform-optimized for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, and Happiness.
- Maintains creator's unique voice while improving quality.
- Learn why certain formats work through AI explanations.

## Internal Rules
- Never position the Creator Engine as "automatic content factory".
- It is a collaborative tool — the creator remains the author.
- Always emphasize the learning aspect: creators improve over time.
